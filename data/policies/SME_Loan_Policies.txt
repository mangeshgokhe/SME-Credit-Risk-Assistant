Policy: Debt-to-Equity and Eligibility
- SMEs with Debt/Equity ratio > 3.0 are high risk and require escalated approval.
- KYC and 2 years financials required.Borrowers
- Loans above 100k require director guarantees.
- SME loans should not exceed 4x annual profit.
- Borrowers with past defaults require enhanced due diligence.
- Company revenue should be stable for at least 2 years.
- Loans over 250k require audited financial statements.
